The Team

Our small but highly competent core group is an ideal combination of skills, ages, expertise, personality and problem solving techniques. We have decades of combined experience in the off-grid sector at various levels ranging from research, software development and marketing, through to project design, hands on system installation and long term maintenance. In addition we have a vast network of industry relevant contacts which we consider of a very high value for the business.

CEO
