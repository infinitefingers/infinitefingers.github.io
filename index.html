Current solutions for
monitoring and control

The off-grid industry is engaged to solve various problems, such as transition from fossil fuels to renewable energy, and ensure a reliable power supply provide electricity to remote locations. To accomplish these goals, millions of Off-Grid power systems will be deployed in the next decades. Off-Grid systems are a complex mix of different technologies. If not properly maintained they tend to fail.

Proper operation and maintenance tools for large fleets of these systems will have to be implemented to avoid these failures. The current solutions for monitoring and control for renewable energy off-grid installations are either prohibitively expensive (e.g. via customised PLC based solutions) or only suitable for a small number of manufacturer-specific systems (eg. inverter manufacturer monitoring solutions).

These manufacturer specific solutions only allow the monitoring of a limited type of system components and are not expandable to include further integrations with other brands or technologies (e.g. water flow meters) that may be also installed in the same system and promote vendor lock-in.
