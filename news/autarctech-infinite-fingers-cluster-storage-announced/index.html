AutarcTech/Infinite Fingers Cluster Storage Announced

  • by Roy Emmerich
  • 2016-04-26

"AutarcTech cluster storage solution"
by Ralf Zenke
license: Copyright AutarcTech

The past few months have been extremely productive. Our collaboration with AutarcTech is starting to produce some really positive results.

Together we’ve taken their 48V Lithium battery balancing board, which has a limited range of applications, added a bit of IT magic and transformed it into a an almost infinitely scaleable storage solution, perfect for industrial applications.

As we are able to, we’ll share more information on the first application of this new product development.
