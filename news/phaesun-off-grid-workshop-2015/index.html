The Phaesun Off-Grid Workshop in September 2015 marks the first public appearance of Infinite Fingers as a business entity.

We took the opportunity offered to us by our strategic partner to attend their workshop and present our ideas to the wide array of businesses active in the off-grid sector.

Thanks Tobias Zwirner, MD of Phaesun GmbH, for this indirect mention of Infinite Fingers in an interview with PV magazine during the Phaesun Off-Grid Workshop 2015:

“We also see these monitoring systems that can work with different components from a whole range of suppliers, putting all system information into one IP software that shows the user a better overview of how their system is performing.”
