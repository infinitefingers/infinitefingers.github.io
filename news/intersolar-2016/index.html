Infinite Fingers presented the Li-ion battery clustering solution in collaboration with Autarctech, during Intersolar 2016.

The Infinite Fingers BMS Master allows multiple standalone AutarcTech LiConnect batteries to be wired up in parallel and be treated as a single battery stack. The Infinite Fingers BMS Master controls all the underlying batteries, aggregating the data and passing it up to the inverter.

For more technical information please visit the AutarcTech website.
